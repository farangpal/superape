ROADMAP MILESTONES

Pre & Public Sale

Only members whose wallets have been whitelisted will be able to mint via pre-sale and public sale. Pre-sale is for those members who have won whitelisted spots via giveaways on Twitter and via roles on Discord. This will be a reserved and guaranteed whitelist. For our public sale whitelist, anyone and everyone can apply for a non-guaranteed whitelist via our discord between 12th and 15th Feb. Our website will randomly pick addresses that will be able to mint on the public sale.

FAQs

What is a Super Ape?

Super Apes are randomly generated, non-fungible tokens that live on the Ethereum blockchain. A total of 10,000 Super Apes have been created of which ninety will be held from sale to use for rewards, giveaways – and for the creators and the team at SAC.

How do I buy a Super Ape?

To purchase a Super Ape Club NFT you will have to be whitelisted for either the pre-sale or public sale. Our presale would be on 15th of Feb, 2022 at 8 PM UTC. Our public sale would be on 17th of Feb, 2022 at 8 PM UTC.

What will be the mint price?

The mint price would be fixed at 0.07 ETH for all members. No pricing tiers, no special pricing – we’re in this for the long run and believe in fair distribution.

Is this just an avatar?

The SAC is a club for those that have made it against all odds. The dark horse – the superhuman. Our concept is all about community – and helping people belong to something that they all can bond over.

What about marketing campaigns?

We are in this for the long haul. Our plans include dedicated PR campaigns, social media ad campaigns, billboards, and a lot more! We will not stop after the first collection – we’ll work on building Enchancted SAC and beyond! We’ll be announcing a lot more plans in the coming future!

Who is the designer of the Super Apes?

The Super Apes have been designed by Luis, known for his Dragon Ball Z artwork and crowded Comic-Con booths.
